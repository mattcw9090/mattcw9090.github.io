MyFinanceTracker

MyFinanceTracker is an iOS application built using SwiftUI and Core Data. It provides individuals and small business owners with an intuitive tool to track net income, manage cash flow, and plan their budget effectively.

Key Functionalities

Net Income Tracking

Instantly view, update, and adjust your net income with each new expense or income entry.

Comprehensive Cash Flow

Keep track of who owes you money and whom you owe, maintaining a clear financial snapshot.

Recurring & Quick Add

Predefine frequent transactions or use “quick add” to streamline repetitive data entry.

Seamless Data Persistence

All data is stored locally with Core Data, ensuring secure and reliable access to your records.
