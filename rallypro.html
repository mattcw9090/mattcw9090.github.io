RallyPro

RallyPro is a powerful tournament management app designed for badminton organizers. It streamlines the process of generating matches, managing player attendance, and tracking results with ease.

Key Features

  • Waitlist and Session Management: Tracks players in the waitlist, current playing session, or not in any session, ensuring smooth organization.
  • Team Assignments: Automatically generates players into teams for the current session, ensuring balanced team compositions.
  • Total Team Score and Player Stats: Calculates team scores and individual player statistics based on match results.
  • Seasonal Statistics: Tracks and aggregates stats for players across all sessions in a season.

How It Works

RallyPro is ideal for tournament organizers looking to manage a seasonal sparring league for players in any sport. Organizers start by adding players to the system, where they can move players between the waitlist and the current playing session. For the current session, players must be assigned to teams with an equal number of members before match fixtures can be generated.

Once the matches are drawn, organizers can input scores for each match. The app then calculates the total team scores and individual player stats for the session. Additionally, RallyPro tracks seasonal stats, providing a comprehensive view of player performance throughout the season.
