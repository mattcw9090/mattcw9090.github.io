10BucksClub

A deal-finder app showcasing local products and experiences for $10 or less, helping users discover budget-friendly options.

Key Features

  • Geo-Location Based Searches
  • Category Filtering for Quick Browsing
  • User Ratings & Reviews for Social Proof
  • Favorite & Share Deals with Friends

Technical Highlights

  • SwiftUI for intuitive UI
  • MVVM Architecture for scalability
  • MapKit Integration for location-based listings
  • Core Data for storing favorites and history
