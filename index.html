About Me

Based in Perth, Western Australia, my journey into iOS development started during my university years when I was tasked with a group project to design a mobile app. I vividly remember spending hours experimenting with code, pushing the limits of what I could create, and eventually realizing how much I enjoyed bringing ideas to life through technology. That experience ignited my passion for app development and inspired me to dive deeper into the Apple developer ecosystem.

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working with a variety of clients, from small startups to established businesses, helping them bring their ideas to life on the Apple App Store. I’m committed to writing clean, maintainable code and staying ahead in the ever-evolving tech world through continuous learning and innovation.

When I’m not immersed in code, you’ll find me playing badminton or working on videography projects. These hobbies help me unwind and keep a balanced approach to life.
