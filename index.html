About the App

MyFinanceTracker is an iOS application I built using SwiftUI and Core Data. It is designed for individuals and small business owners who want a simple yet powerful way to handle their daily finances. With a clean UI and robust functionality, it makes budgeting and financial planning more intuitive.

Key Functionalities

Net Income Tracking

Instantly view, update, and adjust your net income with each new expense or income entry.

Comprehensive Cash Flow

Easily manage who owes you money and whom you owe, ensuring you always know where you stand.

Recurring & Quick Add

Predefine frequent transactions or use the “quick add” feature to streamline repetitive data entry.

Seamless Data Persistence

All data is stored locally with Core Data, keeping your financial records safe and always accessible.
