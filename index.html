MyFinanceTracker

Your personal guide to managing income, expenses, and cash flow with ease.

About the App

MyFinanceTracker is an iOS application designed to help you track net income, manage daily cash flow, and handle recurring transactions effortlessly. Built with SwiftUI and Core Data, it offers a complete in-app experience for budgeting, expense management, and financial oversight.

Key Functionalities

Net Income Tracking

Monitor and adjust your net income in real-time. Add income or expense transactions for any day of the week, and see your net balance update immediately.

Detailed Cash Flow

Keep tabs on who owes you money and whom you owe. Easily add, edit, or remove owed items to maintain clarity over your financial relationships.

Recurring & Quick Add

Set predefined and “quick add” transactions to streamline repetitive entries. Initialize your week with predefined expenses or incomes to save time.

Seamless Data Persistence

All data is stored locally using Core Data, ensuring a smooth offline experience and consistent state across app launches.
