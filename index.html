MyFinanceTracker

MyFinanceTracker is an iOS application built with SwiftUI and Core Data. It’s ideal for individuals and small business owners who want a simple, intuitive tool to manage net income, monitor cash flow, and plan budgets efficiently.

Key Functionalities

Net Income Tracking

Instantly view, update, and adjust net income with every new expense or income entry.

Comprehensive Cash Flow

Manage incoming and outgoing payments effortlessly, ensuring you know your financial standing at all times.

Recurring & Quick Add

Predefine frequent transactions or use the “quick add” feature to simplify repetitive data entry.

Seamless Data Persistence

Utilize Core Data for secure and accessible local data storage, even when offline.
