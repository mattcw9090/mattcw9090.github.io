About Me

I’m based in Perth, Western Australia, and aside from coding, I enjoy hiking, reading about tech trends, and exploring new coffee shops around the city. My journey into iOS development started when I got my first MacBook in college — I instantly fell in love with Swift and the Apple developer ecosystem.

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working with amazing clients, from small startups to established businesses looking to bring their products to the Apple App Store. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and continuously learning to stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech world.

When I’m not coding, you might find me reading up on the latest design patterns, playing the piano, or planning my next travel adventure.
