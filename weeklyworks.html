WeeklyWorks

WeeklyWorks is a comprehensive scheduling app tailored for badminton coaches. It simplifies the process of managing weekly training sessions, venue bookings, and student notifications.

Key Features

  • Session Management: Organize weekly training schedules efficiently.
  • Venue Booking: Automates the reservation process for training venues.
  • Automated Messaging: Notifies students about their sessions via email or SMS.
  • Google Calendar Integration: Ensures schedule synchronization with Google Calendar.
  • Dashboard View: Provides a comprehensive overview for coaches.

How It Works

Coaches can plan their weekly sessions, notify students, and sync events to Google Calendar seamlessly. The app also tracks venue availability and ensures smooth communication with students.
