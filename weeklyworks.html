WeeklyWorks

WeeklyWorks is a comprehensive scheduling app tailored for badminton coaches. It simplifies the process of managing weekly training sessions, venue bookings, and student notifications.

Key Features

  • Weekly Training Management: Organizes and schedules training sessions efficiently for the week.
  • Backend Automation: Automates contacting students and booking courts, reducing administrative workload.
  • API Integration: Integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar to add training events automatically.
  • Finance Integration: Connects with MyFinanceTracker app to add training fees as an income source.

How It Works

WeeklyWorks is designed to streamline scheduling for badminton coaches. Coaches can plan and manage training sessions for the week, while the app handles backend tasks like contacting students about their sessions and booking courts. Integration with Google Calendar ensures schedules are automatically updated and shared.

Furthermore, WeeklyWorks connects with the MyFinanceTracker app to log training fees as income sources, giving coaches a consolidated view of their earnings and schedules. This comprehensive approach saves time and ensures all aspects of weekly training management are handled seamlessly.
